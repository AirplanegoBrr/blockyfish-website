Controls

MOUSE to move

CLICK to boost

ENTER to chat (will reveal your position)

ESC to hide/show menu

A to toggle aimbot

F to toggle chat spammer

M to hide/show chat messages

N to hide/show large map

S to open settings

Hold C to open quick chat pie menu

Hold E to open emoji pie menu

Hold Y to show t8-t10 evolution tree



Slash commands

/ to open chat and use slash commands

/help to view a list of available commands

/mute chat_id to mute a specific player

/unmute chat_id to unmute a specific player

/settarget entity_id to lock on to an entity, useful for multiboxing



Hacks Downloading plugins
  1. Click on the plugins button on the right of Blockyfish Client
  2. Click on get plugins
  3. Download the plugins you want (or all of them)
  4. Go back to the plugins page in Blockyfish Client
  5. Click open plugins folder
  6. Put your downloaded plugins in that folder
  7. Done!
Boost hacks

Hold CTRL and CLICK to use a long charged boost

Hold ALT and CLICK to use a short charged boost

Hold CTRL, SHIFT, and CLICK to use a special charged boost:

  • Walking animals: jump higher than normal
  • Beluga and beaked whale: shoot super speed projectile (uses 2 boosts)
  • Thresher: go nyoom (uses 2 boosts)
Goblin shark 210 dmg

It is possible to glitch goblin shark's charged shot mechanism and fire bullets that deal 210 damage without having to charge for 2 seconds.

Steps:

  1. Charge boost to max. Make sure you reach the -50% speed.
  2. Keep holding down the mouse.
  3. Hold down the control key.
  4. Let go of the mouse.
  5. Hold the control key and click once to fire another shot.

Note that if you do a charged boost, it will cancel the glitch and you will have to do the steps above again.
