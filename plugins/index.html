Blockyfish Plugins

Aim assist

Allows any animal to have mahi mahi's target locking ability. Press A to lock on to a target.

Auto spin

Spin really fast without breaking your arms. Hold Z to activate.

Better vision

Removes flashbangs, ink, and darkness.

Anti-stealth

No more low opacity or invisible animals. Also allows you to see animals that are hiding in props.

No zoom limit

Zoom clamp is removed. You can zoom in or out as far as you want.

I see dead fish

Show all ghosts. Saying "I see dead fish" no longer works.

Slash command revival

Brings back /mute and /unmute. Also adds /settarget for multiboxing.

X-ray vision

See through terrain and ceilings.
